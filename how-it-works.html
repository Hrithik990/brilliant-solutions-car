The Process

Simple as
1, 2, 3

From booking to a brilliant clean — here's exactly what happens at each step of your Brilliant Solutions experience.

01
📱

Book Your Wash

Choose your service package, select a date and time slot that fits your schedule, and share your location in Abu Dhabi. It takes under two minutes.

  • Book via our website form, WhatsApp, or phone call
  • Choose from any available time slot — 7 AM to 9 PM, 7 days
  • Instant confirmation sent to your phone and email
  • Easy rescheduling — no cancellation fees up to 2 hours before
02
🚗

We Come to You

Our professional technician arrives at your building, home, office, or wherever your car is parked — fully equipped with everything needed.

  • Trained, uniformed, and background-verified technician
  • Fully self-contained — brings water, power, and all equipment
  • You receive a live notification 30 minutes before arrival
  • No parking space or access needed — we handle it all
03
✨

We Get to Work

While you go about your day, our technician completes the wash using premium eco-friendly products to the highest professional standard.

  • Biodegradable, car-safe detergents — safe for all paint types
  • Methodical process from roof to sills — nothing is missed
  • Live photo updates if you'd like to see progress
  • Any add-ons applied at this stage as requested
04
💎

Inspect & Enjoy

We notify you when done. You inspect the vehicle, pay conveniently, and drive away in a brilliantly clean car — or just enjoy it where it's parked.

  • You're notified by SMS and WhatsApp when complete
  • Quick walk-around inspection with the technician
  • Pay by cash, card, or bank transfer — your choice
  • Rate us and share feedback via our quick form

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to stay home during the wash?

Not at all. As long as we can access your vehicle in the parking area, you're free to go about your day. We notify you when done.

What if it's raining on my booking day?

If weather conditions affect quality, we'll contact you to reschedule at no charge. We never compromise on results.

Do you need access to water at my location?

No — our technicians carry their own water tank. We are completely self-sufficient. No tap or power supply needed from your side.

How long does each service take?

Express: 20–30 min. Standard Detail: 45–60 min. Premium Detail: 90–120 min. Paint Protection: 3–5 hours depending on the vehicle.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, credit/debit card, and bank transfer. Payment is collected after the service is completed to your satisfaction.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We'll redo the service at no extra cost — no arguments, no hassle. Your satisfaction is our guarantee on every single wash.

Can I book for multiple cars?

Yes — simply mention this when booking. We can service multiple vehicles in one visit with a small multi-car discount applied automatically.

How do I modify or cancel a booking?

Contact us via WhatsApp or phone. Cancellations or changes are free up to 2 hours before your scheduled appointment.

Ready to Book?

It takes under 2 minutes. We'll confirm within 30 minutes and handle everything else.
