Who We Are

A Local Business
with Global Standards

Brilliant Solutions Car Wash was founded in Abu Dhabi in 2020 with a simple mission: make professional car care effortless. We saw that busy residents and professionals in the UAE didn't have time to drive to a car wash and wait in line — so we brought the service to them.

What started as a small team of three has grown into Abu Dhabi's most trusted door-to-door car wash service, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across all major districts. We combine the professionalism of a luxury detailing studio with the convenience of on-demand delivery.

Every product we use is carefully selected to be effective, safe for your vehicle's finish, and gentle on the environment — critical values for us as an Abu Dhabi business.
